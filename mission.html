Mission

The mission of this blog is to provide honest, engaging information regarding my personal experiences as a data engineer, as a post graduate, and as a queer woman of color.

All of my published content here is free, open, and honest. I built this blog by writing custom code (with the help of an MIT license), which is publically available for anyone to copy, use, test.

No ads, no paid memberships. Just honesty.

My goal is to provide anyone with access to information that can be helpful for anyone interested in becoming a data engineer. I will never accept any paid advertisements nor will ask for any paid memberships.

My reviews are honest and personal. Any recommendations I provide have supported my own experience in becoming a data engineer.

Do I make money from this blog?

Nope.
